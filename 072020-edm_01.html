Things to remember
 
1. This is a live pilot.
You’ll be linking Jarvis to your own Metrobank account and saving with your own money. Make sure to learn and understand all the features for a good experience!
 
2. Keep it quiet.
At the moment, Jarvis is limited to Metrobank employees. Please keep all information about it strictly confidential. Please don’t share the app with people outside the bank, or post about it on social media.
 
3. We’d love to hear from you.
This is a beta release. We know there are are still bugs, and we're trying to catch them all before we release Jarvis to the public. This is where we need your help. If you catch a bug, encounter an issue, or have any feedback, please share it with us at pilot@earnest.ph.


You can also share your experience and get more information in the Project Jarvis group on Workplace. You can visit it here: metrobank.workplace.com/groups/275763026954020.
